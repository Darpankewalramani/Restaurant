FKDH Restaurant

Our Restaurant is a state of the art Restaurant named on the first initials of its owners, Darpan Kumar Kewalramani and Eshwar Kumar Kewalramani. This Restaurant was made with much hardwork, love and compassion. Its interior decoration was done by renowned foriegn designers. Our infrastructure was also built by designers for a touch of luxurious experience. Our Kitchen is was also specially designed with all the imported machineries, kitchen items, outline and representation. Our kitchens are 24/7 open for customers so they can check the proper precautions we take regarding hygiene, our imported and fresh food stocks, and our disciplined and highly experienced staff. We follow all the SOP's instructed by our government.
